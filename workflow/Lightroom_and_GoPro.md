# Lightroom and GOPRO

Let's configure the GoPro 8 to shoot a nighlapse

* Time Lapse => Night lapse
* Lens: Wide
* Interval: Auto
* Format: Photo
* Output: RAW
* Shutter: 30s
* White Balance: Native
* ISO Min: 100
* ISO Max: 800
* Sharpness: Medium
* Color: Flat
